# Google Play Console Notes

Last updated: 2026-03-21

This document is a project checklist for preparing Zip Puzzle Studio for Google Play submission.

## Store Listing Direction

- App name: `Zip Puzzle Studio`
- Short description should describe it as an original pathfinding puzzle game.
- Avoid references that imply affiliation with another game, brand, ranking, or promotion program.
- Use only original screenshots, icon art, feature graphic, and descriptive text created for this project.

## Audience and Content

- Position the app as a general-audience puzzle game.
- Avoid sexual content, gambling themes, hate content, shocking imagery, or misleading claims in gameplay or store assets.
- Keep the experience playable without requiring personal data from the user.

## Privacy and Data Safety

- Host a public privacy policy URL before submission.
- Current draft policy file: `docs/privacy_policy.md`
- If the shipping app remains local-only with no analytics, ads, login, or tracking, reflect that accurately in the Data safety form.
- Re-review Data safety answers before every release if SDKs or services change.

## App Behavior

- Ensure the app launches reliably and remains playable without deceptive flows.
- Do not add intrusive ads, forced redirects, fake system warnings, or install prompts.
- Keep the app functional for users without requiring unnecessary permissions.

## Play Console Metadata Reminder

- Add support contact email.
- Add privacy policy URL.
- Add clear app category and general-audience disclosures.
- Upload only original marketing assets and honest feature descriptions.
